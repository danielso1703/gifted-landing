About Top Notch Gifts

Top Notch Gifts is a search and discovery tool helping you find thoughtful gift ideas for everyone on your list.

What We Do

We help people discover gift ideas by recipient type, age, budget, and occasion. Whether you're shopping for a birthday, holiday, or special milestone, our goal is to make the process easier and more inspiring.

Our Goal

Gift shopping can be stressful and time-consuming. We want to help you find thoughtful gifts faster, reduce that stress, and save you time. We aim to highlight unique ideas you might not have found on your own.

How We’re Different

We focus on curated discovery, trending items, and helpful guides. We are not a marketplace—we don't sell products directly. Instead, we organize options from various retailers to give you the best selection in one place.

How We Make Money

Top Notch Gifts is a content and referral site. We may earn commissions through affiliate links when you click out to a retailer and make a purchase. This comes at no extra cost to you. For more details, please read our Affiliate Disclosure.

Where We Operate

We are currently based in Toronto, Ontario, and operate with a Canada-first approach, with plans to expand our reach over time.

Contact Us

Have questions or feedback? We'd love to hear from you. Visit our Contact page to get in touch.
