FAQ

What is Top Notch Gifts?

Top Notch Gifts is a gift search and discovery tool that helps you discover the perfect gifts for your loved ones. You set who you’re shopping for, their age range, and your budget; we show you a search results of ideas from multiple retailers. You can save searches and click to shop.

Which stores are supported?

Top Notch Gifts surfaces products from multiple retailers, including eBay, Etsy, Walmart, and others. Product data is refreshed regularly. When you click to shop, you’re taken to the retailer’s site to complete your purchase.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use encryption (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication (Google/Apple OAuth), and store data in Supabase with row-level security. We don’t sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do affiliate links work?

Some links in Top Notch Gifts are affiliate links. If you make a purchase after clicking through, we may receive a commission from the retailer or its affiliate network (e.g. Rakuten Advertising for Walmart Canada). This doesn’t change the price you pay. See our Affiliate Disclosure for more.

How can I contact you?

For support or inquiries: team@topnotchgifts.ca.
