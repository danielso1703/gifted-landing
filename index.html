FAQ

What is Gifted?

Gifted is a mobile app that helps you discover the perfect gifts for your loved ones. You set who you’re shopping for, their age range, and your budget; we show you a personalized feed of ideas from multiple retailers. You can save searches, share them with friends, and tap through to buy.

Which stores are supported?

Gifted surfaces products from multiple retailers, including eBay, Etsy, Walmart, and others. Product data is refreshed regularly. When you tap to shop, you’re taken to the retailer’s site to complete your purchase.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use encryption (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication (Google/Apple OAuth), and store data in Supabase with row-level security. We don’t sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do affiliate links work?

Some links in Gifted are affiliate links. If you make a purchase after clicking through, we may receive a commission from the retailer or its affiliate network (e.g. Rakuten Advertising for Walmart Canada). This doesn’t change the price you pay. See our Affiliate Disclosure for more.
